Frequently asked questions

Use text search, voice search, image-assisted search and category browsing to find products quickly. Product pages include price, delivery charge, stock, SKU and tags.

How do I use voice search?

Open Products, press the microphone button, speak a product name or category, and the catalog filters instantly.

How does image-assisted search work?

Upload an image, then add a visual hint such as color or item type. The browser matches against product names, tags and categories.

How do I change prices easily?

Edit base price, commission percent/fixed value and delivery charge in the CSV or Google Apps Script sheet. Final prices recalculate from those fields.

What is AEO/GEO optimization here?

Pages include answer-style summaries, FAQ schema, Product schema, category intent pages and clean snippets so search and AI systems can understand product facts.
