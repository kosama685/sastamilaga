📋 How to Submit Sitemap to Google

  1. Download the XML sitemap above
  2. Upload it to your web server root: https://sastamilaga.com/sitemap.xml
  3. Go to Google Search Console
  4. Select your property → Sitemaps (left menu)
  5. Enter sitemap.xml and click Submit
  6. Google will crawl your sitemap within 24–72 hours
  7. Also add to robots.txt: Sitemap: https://sastamilaga.com/sitemap.xml
  8. Re-submit whenever you add new products
💡 Pro Tip: Since this is a static SPA, also consider using prerender.io or Next.js for full server-side rendering so Google can index each product page directly.
